Tool & Die Maker
"Everything spins around precision. Including me."
Gear-ring body ⚙️ Dual rotating rings Big safety glasses

Gauge lives inside two counter-rotating gear rings — always at the center, always in control. Squat and unflappable. The kind of character who's seen every tolerance violation and judges them all.

Mold Setter
SETTER
Mold Setter
"Two halves. One perfect part."
Split mold body 🪨 Cooling channels Glowing cavity Parting line seam

Setter's body IS the mold — two halves that rhythmically open and close, revealing a glowing injection cavity at their core. Eyes on each half, mouth on the parting line. Cooling channels and ejector pins as decoration.

Quality
SPEC
Quality Inspector
"I see everything. Everything."
Magnifier body 🔍 Face in lens Roving scan anim Scrutinizing brows

Spec's entire body is a magnifying glass. The face lives inside the lens — raised eyebrows permanently in judgment mode. The handle doubles as legs. Roves around the viewport looking for defects.

SIGMA
Process Engineer
"The trend line never lies. Usually."
Chart body 📈 Live trend line Floating head Round glasses ☕

Sigma's torso and legs are replaced by a live bar chart with an animated trend line. The head floats above the data. Coffee in hand, glasses on — always analyzing, never quite finished.

Forklift
LIFT
Forklift Operator
"I don't drive the forklift. I am the forklift."
Centaur chassis 🚜 Raising forks Spinning wheels Hi-vis torso

Lift is a forklift centaur — human torso and head riding directly on a forklift chassis instead of legs. Forks raise independently, wheels spin while bouncing. The most chaotic energy on the floor.

Concept Notes

Each character is built from a concept specific to their role rather than a shared body template: Torch is a living flame, Gauge lives inside rotating gears, Setter's body IS the mold halves, Spec's body IS the magnifier, Sigma has a bar chart for a torso, and Lift is a forklift centaur. Role colors from v1 are preserved throughout.
