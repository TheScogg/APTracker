Built-In Tools

Everything your team needs

A complete factory floor toolkit running entirely in the browser — no app to install, no server to manage.

📡
Real-Time Sync
Firebase Firestore powers live updates across every device on the floor. Issues appear the moment they're logged — no refresh, no polling.
📸
Photo Capture
Attach photos straight from your phone's camera or gallery. Stored full-resolution in Firebase Storage, instantly viewable by the whole team.
📄
PDF Export
Export any filtered issue view to a formatted PDF report — photos, timelines, and timestamps included. Perfect for shift handoffs and audits.
🔐
Role-Based Access
Admin, Editor, and Viewer roles per plant. Granular permissions control who can create issues, resolve them, manage statuses, or export data.
📱
Mobile-First Design
Built for gloved hands and bright factory floors. Big touch targets, swipe gestures for fast status changes, and a layout that works on any screen.
🎨
6 Themes
Dark mode by default, plus Light, Forest, Ocean, Sunset, and Royal themes — toggle from the user menu to match your environment or preference.
🏭
Multi-Plant
Manage unlimited plants from one login. Each plant has its own press layout, status config, member list, and issue history. Switch with one tap.
📋
Append-Only Event Log
Every status change is written to an immutable events subcollection. Full audit trail from open to close — nothing gets overwritten or lost.
⚡
Cloudflare Workers
Deployed as a static site on Cloudflare's global edge network — loads instantly from anywhere on the plant floor, even on weak Wi-Fi.
